What is DSE?

DSE is a project created in 2022 bound to create NFT's to change the world forever.

Three young aspiring students at the Kantonsschule Zug, Erik Balabanov, Rajitpal Singh, Gian Meier, decided to change the NFT space.
How you might ask? Well NFT's are digital art pieces. But art is defined by a piece having a value behing it, resembling a belief and nowadays we lost it in the NFT space.
We at DSE create NFT's to talk about world wide concerning PROBLEMS (Climate Change, Pollution, etc.). Our motive was never to make money but to help humanity to sustain humanity.
That's also the reason why we decided to donate min. 20 % of all Revenue generated by the purchasement of our NFT's on the world famous plattform OpenSea.
